ArqOptimize™ (FinSecOps)

25-40% Cost Reduction in 90 Days 60-Day Payback Period 400%+ ROI Over 2 Years Zero "Zombie Resources" Left Running

Enterprises waste up to 30-40% of cloud spend on idle resources. ArqOptimize connects your project tools (Jira, Asana, Trello, Monday) with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) to detect and eliminate orphaned assets automatically turning project closure into instant savings.

ArqEstate™ (Real Estate Sales Enablement)

Zero unauthorized access 100% traceable assignments Automated regulatory compliance Real-time leadership visibility

Real estate operations scaled with governance built in. ArqEstate ensures every lead is assigned to the correct agent-of-record, enforces jurisdictional compliance, prevents listing violations, and maintains complete audit trails for commission disputes. Built on Compliance-Aware Prompt Compiler™.

70% faster screening 100% consistent scoring Complete audit trails Multi-jurisdiction compliance

Investment teams conduct thorough, repeatable due diligence without sacrificing speed. ArqIntel ingests documents from any source, applies consistent analysis frameworks, scores risks within your criteria, and produces audit-ready reports all with governance enforced at every step.
